Breakfast Burrito

An easy recipe for the most important meal of the day!

If you're wondering how to make an amazing breakfast burrito we've got you covered. Whether you're new to making breakfast burritos or have made many in the past, our recipe is sure to impress even the most critical foodies. Made from some of the most basic ingredients in any kitchen, our quick and simple breakfast burrito recipe will always be a great option when the creative juices aren't flowing.

With ingredients like potatoes, bacon, and eggs, our breakfast burrito recipe doesn't throw any curveballs. Best of all, if you're vegan or vegatarian you'll find it easy to omit certain ingredients while keeping your meal delicious!

Ingredients for 3 burritos

Stuffings

  • 3, 12" tortilla or bigger
  • 2 Russet potatoes
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 cup Shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 lb of bacon
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Yellow onions
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 2 cups of spinach
  • Salsa of choice

Seasonings

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water as you cut the potatoes into small squares. Place the potatoes in the pot once it is boiling.
  2. Cut bacon into small slices and cook on medium head in a cast iron skillet until crispy. When complete, remove bacon from the skillet and add the potatoes after they have boiled for 10 minutes.
  3. Begin to dice the onion and red bell pepper. Once diced, add to skillet.
  4. Once potatoes, onions, and bell peppers begin to brown and caramelize, add eggs and scramble into the mix.
  5. Once eggs are cooked, add seasonings to taste and remove from heat. Keep stove at medium setting.
  6. Heat tortillas in a microwave for 10 seconds to soften them for rolling. Slice the avocado.
  7. In the following order, add the spinach, avocado, salsa, egg and vegetable mix, cheese, and bacon to the tortilla. Fold the sides and roll.
  8. Grill each rolled burrito on the skillet for 1-2 minutes on both sides, checking occassionally for a golden-brown crisp.
  9. Remove from heat and serve!
